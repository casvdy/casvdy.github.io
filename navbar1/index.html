About the College

Established in 1999, the College of Applied Science Vadakkencherry has been a pioneer in providing quality education in basic science and computer applications. Affiliated to the University of Calicut, we offer undergraduate programs designed to meet the evolving demands of the modern workforce.

Our institution boasts modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and experienced faculty members dedicated to student success. We emphasize holistic development through various extracurricular activities and industry partnerships.

Recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) and 12(B), the college maintains high academic standards while fostering an inclusive environment for students from diverse backgrounds.
