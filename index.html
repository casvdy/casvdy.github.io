About the College

College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry, established in 1993, is a premier government institution under IHRD Kerala, offering advanced education in technology, commerce, and applied sciences. Affiliated with the University of Calicut and recognized by UGC & AICTE, we provide industry-driven programs such as B.Sc Computer Science, BCA, M.Sc Electronics, and specialized commerce courses. Equipped with modern labs, expert faculty, and a dynamic placement cell, we bridge academic rigor with career success.

College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry welcomes all kinds of students who love to reach their full potential while being in a beneficial environment, broadening your horizons. This is the place where ambition meets reality.

At College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry, we shape tomorrow’s leaders—where knowledge meets real-world impact. Invest in excellence. Lead the future.
