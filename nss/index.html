National Service Scheme (NSS)
National Service Scheme (NSS) - Units 77 & 78


Number of Units

Our college proudly hosts two active NSS units: Unit 77 and Unit 78, dedicated to fostering social responsibility and community engagement among students.

Flagship Programmes
The NSS units of our college undertake various flagship programmes that align with national and state-level initiatives, including:

• Swachh Bharat Abhiyan – Cleanliness drives in and around the campus.

• Green Campus Initiative – Tree plantation and sustainable environmental practices.

• Blood Donation Camps – Encouraging students and faculty to donate blood for a noble cause.

• Health and Wellness Camps – Free medical check-ups and awareness campaigns.

• Literacy & Skill Development Programmes – Teaching and training sessions for underprivileged communities.

• Disaster Relief and Rehabilitation – Volunteering during natural calamities.
