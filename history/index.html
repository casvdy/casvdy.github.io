College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry

HISTORY
Introduction
Established in 1993, the College of Applied Science (COLLEGE OF APPLIED SCIENCE, VADAKKENCHERRY) in Vadakkencherry, Palakkad district, Kerala, India, stands as a pioneering government institution under the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a Government of Kerala undertaking. Affiliated with the University of Calicut and recognized under Section 2F of the UGC and AICTE, College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry embodies its motto, “Think Globally, Act Globally,” through its commitment to innovative education in applied sciences and commerce.

Founding and Early History
College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry was founded with a vision to deliver specialized undergraduate programs in emerging fields. Situated along National Highway 544, approximately 33 km from both Palakkad and Thrissur, the college’s strategic location enhances accessibility for students across the region.

Academic Expansion
Over the decades, College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry expanded its academic portfolio to include postgraduate programs and specialized diplomas. A landmark achievement came in 2010 with the launch of Kerala’s first government-level Postgraduate Diploma in Audio Engineering, positioning the college as the fourth institution in India to offer such a program, which has been now discontinued. This initiative underscored College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry’s dedication to addressing industry demands and fostering niche skill sets.

Academic Programs
The college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses under the semester system, aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) of India. Key programs include:
- Undergraduate (3 years): B.Sc. Computer Science, BCA, B.Sc. Electronics, B.Com with Computer Applications, and B.Com with Finance.
- Postgraduate (2 years): M.Sc. Computer Science, M.Sc. Electronics, and M.Com.

Campus and Facilities
The college’s permanent campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced Computer and Electronics Laboratories, a well-equipped library, and modern classrooms. These resources support hands-on learning and research, critical for applied science disciplines.

Student Life and Associations
Student-led departmental associations—CASCA (Computer Science), PULSE (Electronics), and VOC (Commerce)—play a vital role in fostering academic and extracurricular engagement. Each association, guided by faculty advisors, organizes workshops, seminars, and technical events, cultivating leadership and collaborative skills among students.

Placement and Career Development
College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry’s proactive Placement Cell bridges academia and industry, offering training programs, real-time workshops, and mock interviews to enhance employability. Collaborations with corporate partners enable campus recruitment drives, while personality development sessions led by industry experts prepare students for competitive environments.

Leadership and Governance
Under the stewardship of Principal Dr. Pradip Somasundaran, College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry continues to uphold its legacy of excellence. Governed by IHRD, the college remains dedicated to nurturing globally competent professionals, contributing to Kerala’s educational and technological advancement.
