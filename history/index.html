College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry

HISTORY
**College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry (CAS VDY): A Historical Overview** **Introduction** Established in 1993, the College of Applied Science (CAS VDY) in Vadakkencherry, Palakkad district, Kerala, India, stands as a pioneering government institution under the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a Government of Kerala undertaking. Affiliated with the University of Calicut and recognized under Section 2F of the UGC and AICTE, CAS VDY embodies its motto, *“Think Globally, Act Globally,”* through its commitment to innovative education in applied sciences and commerce. **Founding and Early History** CAS VDY was founded with a vision to deliver specialized undergraduate programs in emerging fields. It made history as the first college in Kerala to introduce B.Sc. Electronics and Computer Science, reflecting its forward-thinking approach to technology education. Situated along National Highway 47, approximately 33 km from both Palakkad and Thrissur, the college’s strategic location enhances accessibility for students across the region. **Academic Expansion** Over the decades, CAS VDY expanded its academic portfolio to include postgraduate programs and specialized diplomas. A landmark achievement came in 2010 with the launch of Kerala’s first government-level Postgraduate Diploma in Audio Engineering, positioning the college as the fourth institution in India to offer such a program. This initiative underscored CAS VDY’s dedication to addressing industry demands and fostering niche skill sets. **Academic Programs** The college offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses under the semester system, aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) of India. Key programs include: - **Undergraduate (3 years):** B.Sc. Computer Science, BCA, B.Sc. Electronics, B.Com with Computer Applications, and B.Com with Finance. - **Postgraduate (2 years):** M.Sc. Computer Science, M.Sc. Electronics, and M.Com. With fees ranging from approximately ₹13,035 to ₹22,550 per semester, these programs combine affordability with academic rigor, attracting over 400 students annually. **Campus and Facilities** The college’s permanent campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including advanced Computer and Electronics Laboratories, a well-equipped library, and modern classrooms. These resources support hands-on learning and research, critical for applied science disciplines. **Student Life and Associations** Student-led departmental associations—CASCA (Computer Science), PULSE (Electronics), and VOC (Commerce)—play a vital role in fostering academic and extracurricular engagement. Each association, guided by faculty advisors, organizes workshops, seminars, and technical events, cultivating leadership and collaborative skills among students. **Placement and Career Development** CAS VDY’s proactive Placement Cell bridges academia and industry, offering training programs, real-time workshops, and mock interviews to enhance employability. Collaborations with corporate partners enable campus recruitment drives, while personality development sessions led by industry experts prepare students for competitive environments. **Leadership and Governance** Under the stewardship of Principal Dr. Pradip Somasundaran (M.Sc. Tech, M.Phil), CAS VDY continues to uphold its legacy of excellence. Governed by IHRD, the college remains dedicated to nurturing globally competent professionals, contributing to Kerala’s educational and technological advancement. **Current Status** Today, CAS VDY enrolls 402 students, with 264 undergraduates and 138 postgraduates, reflecting its balanced focus on foundational and advanced education. As it evolves with emerging academic trends, the college remains a beacon of innovation in applied sciences and commerce, anchored by its mission to empower students for global challenges.
