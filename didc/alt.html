DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE & DEVELOPMENT COMMITTEE (DIDC)

Digital Infrastructure and Development Committee is one of the most active and talented team of students at College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry, overseeing the website, student login and other digital technologies that both the students and the staff of the college benefit from. DIDC was founded on 18 February 2025 by Aswin S Nair and Basil Shybu with the supervision from college faculty to rebuild the college website from scratch and to bring advanced college facilities.
