Digital Infrastructure &
    Development Committee

A student-led team dedicated to building and managing the essential technologies that connect students and staff at the College of Applied Science, Vadakkencherry.

The DIDC was founded on 18 February 2025 by students Aswin S Nair and Basil Shybu, under the guidance of college faculty, with the ambitious goal of rebuilding the college's digital infrastructure from the ground up.
